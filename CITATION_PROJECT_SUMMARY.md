## CITATION METHODOLOGY

### Source Priority Order

1. **training.gov.au** - PRIMARY source for unit requirements
   - Unit descriptor and application
   - Elements and performance criteria
   - Foundation skills
   - Assessment requirements

2. **Safe Work Australia** - SECONDARY for safety/compliance
   - Model WHS Regulations
   - Model Codes of Practice
   - Guidance materials

3. **State/Territory Regulators** - TERTIARY for jurisdiction-specific
   - Licensing procedures
   - Forms and applications
   - Local variations

4. **Australian Standards** - REFERENCE ONLY (copyright)
   - Cite standard number and title
   - DO NOT quote standard text
   - Direct readers to purchase from SAI Global

---

### Citation Placement Rules

**MUST Cite:**
✅ Regulatory requirements ("must", "required")
✅ Performance criteria from units
✅ Safety procedures and guidelines
✅ Risk management processes
✅ Licensing and legal requirements
✅ Technical specifications
✅ Emergency procedures

**DO NOT Need to Cite:**
❌ General workplace practices
❌ Common sense safety
❌ Manufacturer-specific information (cite manufacturer instead)
❌ Generic definitions (unless from official source)
