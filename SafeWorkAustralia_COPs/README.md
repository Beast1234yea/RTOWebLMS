## ğŸ“š WHAT ARE MODEL CODES OF PRACTICE?

Model Codes of Practice (COPs) are official guidance documents published by Safe Work Australia that provide practical advice on how to comply with the Model Work Health and Safety (WHS) Regulations.

**Key Points:**
- âœ… Free to download and use
- âœ… Non-copyright Australian Government publications
- âœ… Can be included in RTO training materials
- âœ… Can be used for AI tutor training
- âœ… Regularly updated by Safe Work Australia

**Legal Status:**
- Model COPs are **admissible in court** as evidence of what is known about a hazard, risk, or control
- Following a COP is considered meeting the WHS duty (though not mandatory)
- Alternative methods allowed if same or higher safety standard achieved
