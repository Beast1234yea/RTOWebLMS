## CITATION STRATEGY BY PRIORITY

### Priority 1: HIGH RISK WORK LICENCES (HRWL) - IMMEDIATE

**Units:** TLILIC series, CPCCLRG series, RIIWHS204E, HLTAID011

**Why:** These require HRWL or nationally recognised certification. Regulatory requirements are strict. Incorrect information could lead to compliance issues.

**Primary Sources Required:**
- training.gov.au unit pages
- Safe Work Australia Model WHS Regulations Part 4.5 (High risk work)
- Safe Work Australia Codes of Practice (Plant, Work at Heights, First Aid)
- State/territory licensing authorities

**Estimated Pages:** ~2,000+ pages across 11 unique units

**Agent Assignment:** Deploy 3-4 specialized agents working in parallel

---

### Priority 2: RESOURCES & INFRASTRUCTURE UNITS - IMPORTANT

**Units:** RIIMPO series, RIIHAN305D, RIIPRO301D, RIISS00054, MSMWHS217

**Why:** Industry-specific units with specific safety and operational requirements. May not require HRWL but still nationally recognised and compliance-critical.

**Primary Sources Required:**
- training.gov.au unit pages
- Safe Work Australia general guidance
- Industry-specific codes (mining, resources)
- State mining safety regulators (where applicable)

**Estimated Pages:** ~800+ pages across 6 unique units

**Agent Assignment:** Deploy 2 specialized agents

---

### Priority 3: GENERAL MOBILE PLANT OPERATIONS - STANDARD

**Units:** Dozer, Telehandler, Backhoe, Tool Carrier, Spotter, Vehicle Loading Crane

**Why:** Equipment-specific training, may or may not map to specific units. Still requires proper safety citations.

**Primary Sources Required:**
- Identify unit codes (if applicable)
- Safe Work Australia plant safety guidance
- Generic mobile plant safety requirements

**Estimated Pages:** ~500+ pages

**Agent Assignment:** Deploy 1-2 agents
